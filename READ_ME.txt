This website allows users to browse books by title and place an order to have those books delivered to their local bookstore for pickup.
“Book Worm” offers login options for both customers and admins alike. 
As a customer, you are able to browse books by title and place an order to have those books delivered to their local bookstore for pickup. 
Customers are also able to submit feedback about their experience that can later be reviewed by admins. 
Admins have the ability to change the website theme as well as keep track of the current stock of books within the database.
